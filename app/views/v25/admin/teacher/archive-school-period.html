Archiving a school period allows undoing a registration that was made in error.

! Warning Only continue if the registration was made in error.

What will happen

  • the selected school period will be archived
  • if John Smith has only one school period and there are no declarations, the teacher record will also be archived
  • other school periods and registrations will not be affected unless you choose to archive them separately

If the teacher is registered as an ECT and a mentor

A teacher can be registered as both an ECT and a mentor. Only one of those registrations may have been made in error.

Check which registration is incorrect before you continue. You can archive the school period for the registration that was made in error without affecting the other.

If the ECT is on a provider-led programme

Before you continue, contact the lead provider offline to tell them you are archiving this registration.
