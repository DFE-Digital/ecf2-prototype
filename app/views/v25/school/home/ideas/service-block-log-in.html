We're blocking access to some activities in the service from 1 June to 15 June 2026

From 1 June to 15 June you will not be able to use the service to:

  • register new early career teachers (ECTs) and mentors
  • change ECT training programmes

Why we're doing this

We have not yet opened registration for ECTs starting in the 2026 to 2027 academic year. We expect to do this 15 June.

If you're registering an ECT late

You should:

  1. Contact the ECT's lead provider now so that they can start to set up the ECT's training.
  2. Register the ECT in this service when registration opens on 15 June.
