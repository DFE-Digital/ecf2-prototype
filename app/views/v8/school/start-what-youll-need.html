For ECTs and mentors you'll need to provide these details:

  • name
  • teacher reference number (TRN)
  • date of birth
  • email address
  • lead provider

For ECTs only, you'll also need to provide these details:

  • start date at your school as an ECT
  • full or part time working pattern
  • date of birth
  • appropriate body
  • provider-led or school-led training
What are the roles of an appropriate body, lead provider and delivery partner?

An appropriate body is responsible for assuring the quality of the statutory induction of ECTs. A lead provider creates the materials used for training ECTs and mentors, while the delivery partner delivers training events.

These roles are sometimes undertaken by the same organisation, for example an appropriate body might be the same organisation as the delivery partner.
