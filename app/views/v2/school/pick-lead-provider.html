What is a lead provider?

They are organisations that:

  • create the overall structure, content and resources for early career framework (ECF) training
  • ensure that the training delivered by the delivery partner is of a high quality and meets national standards
  • train mentors and trainers who deliver the ECF to early career teachers
  • report on the progress and effectiveness of the training programme to the Department for Education (DfE)
