What is an appropriate body?

They are organisations that:

  • check schools are following the correct procedures for ECTs and that the support provided meets national standards
  • monitor the progress of ECTs during their induction period
  • decide if an ECT should pass or fail their induction after assessing whether they have met the teachers' standards (at end of induction period)
  • advise schools on how to improve an ECTs support if there are any problems to resolve
