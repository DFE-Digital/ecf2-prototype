Use this service to register early career teachers (ECTs) and mentors for training as part of their early career teacher entitlement (ECTE).

You need to register ECTs and mentors so that:

  • they can access ECTE training
  • your school receives funding to support their time off timetable for training and mentoring

What your school needs to have in place

Before you can register ECTs and mentors with this this service, your school needs to have certain things in place to support ECTE.

Your school needs to have:

  • appointed an appropriate body
  • decided on the training programme it wants to use (provider or school-led)
  • partnered with a lead provider (if using a provider-led training programme)

Read our guidance on setting up and managing ECTE in your school.

What you need to register ECTs and mentors

You need to tell us about your ECTs and mentors. Find out what information you need to provide.
