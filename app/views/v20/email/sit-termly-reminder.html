Termly reminder to induction tutor

When

At the beginning of each school term.

Why

A reminder to school induction tutors to tell DfE about new participants and changes.

Subject: Reminder to tell the DfE about early career teacher and mentor changes

Hello ((sit_name)),

We’re sending you this email as our records show that you’re the induction tutor for ((school_name)).

This is a reminder to tell us about ECT and mentor changes this term.

You need to tell us about changes so that:

  • your school gets the correct funding
  • early career teachers (ECTs) and mentors can get access to training and materials

Tell us about changes with register early career teachers.

What you need to tell us

New ECTs and mentors

You need to register:

  • ECTs starting their early career teacher entitlement (ECTE)
  • mentors assigned to ECTs and who are starting their ECT mentor training programme
  • ECTs and mentors who’ve moved to your school from another

Changes with training and induction programmes

Tell us if:

  • ECTs or mentors change training programme or training provider
  • ECTs change their appropriate body
  • ECTs change mentor

ECTs or mentors leaving

Tell us if ECTs or mentors are leaving your school and not expected to return.

Changes in personal details

Tell us if ECTs or mentors change their:

  • name
  • email
  • working pattern

Regards,

Teacher Continuing Professional Development

Department for Education
