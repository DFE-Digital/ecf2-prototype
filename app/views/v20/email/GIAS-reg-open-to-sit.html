Registration open to GIAS contact

When

June

Why

To give notification that registration is open. This email will follow the advance notice email. It highlights:

  • a different way to sign in to the service and the need for a DfE sign-in account
  • the need to confirm the induction tutor for the academic year
  • what the GIAS contact needs to do
Subject: Registration for early career teachers and mentors open for academic year 2026-2027

Hello ((GIAS_contact_name)),

Our records show that ((sit_name)) (((sit_email-address))) is your school’s induction tutor. We’ve tried to contact them to let them know that registration for early career teachers (ECTs) and mentors is open, but we’ve been unsuccessful.

What you need to do

Forward this email to your school’s induction tutor. It explains how to register ECTS and mentors for training.

If your school does not have an induction tutor, choose one and forward this email to them.

You can read about choosing an induction tutor in our Set up and manage the early career teacher entitlement guidance.

Register ECTs and mentors

You can now register early career teachers and mentors for the 2026-2027 academic year with register early career teachers.

To use the service you’ll need:

  • a DfE Sign-in account
  • approved access to the service

DfE Sign-in account and service access

If you do not have a DfE Sign-in account, you can create one.

If you do not have access to the service, you can request it from your school or organisation’s approver using your DfE Sign-in account.

Confirm your school’s induction tutor for the 2026-2027 academic year

When you first register ECTs and mentors for this academic year, we’ll ask you to confirm if you’ll be the induction tutor, or if it will be someone else.
