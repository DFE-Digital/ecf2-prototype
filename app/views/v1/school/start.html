Use this service to register your school’s ECTs for the early career framework (ECF) training.

You need to register your ECTs and their mentors with the Department for Education (DfE) so that they can get:

  • access to DfE-funded training
  • funding to support their time off timetable for mentoring and training

What you’ll need

You’ll need to provide details about the:

  • early career teacher (ECT) and their mentor
  • appropriate body you’ve selected
  • training option chosen

You must provide these details before your ECTs can start the ECF training.

If any of these details change, you can use this service to update them.

You'll need to sign in using DfE Sign-in.
