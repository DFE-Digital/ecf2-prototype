Personal information

You'll need the ECT's:

  • name
  • teacher reference number (TRN)
  • date of birth
  • email address
  • start date at your school (as an ECT)
  • appropriate body (who help assess their induction)

Training details

You must tell us about your choice of:

  • programme type
  • lead provider
  • delivery partner
  • mentor

If you've not assigned a mentor for your ECT, you can still register them by providing the mentor details later.
